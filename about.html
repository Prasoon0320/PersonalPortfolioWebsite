I'm a Competitive Coder.

A competitive programmer possesses a diverse set of skills that enable them to excel in algorithmic challenges and coding competitions. Firstly, they have a deep understanding of data structures, including arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, and graphs. This knowledge allows them to efficiently manipulate and organize data to solve complex problems. Additionally, competitive programmers are adept at designing and implementing algorithms, ranging from basic sorting and searching to advanced techniques like dynamic programming and graph traversal. They excel in problem-solving and critical thinking, often breaking down complex tasks into smaller, manageable steps.
